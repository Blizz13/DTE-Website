About Us

The Rototuna Chess Club was established to bring together chess enthusiasts of all skill levels from the community. Our mission is to promote the game of chess through regular meetups, tournaments, and educational programs. Whether you're a beginner looking to learn the basics or a seasoned player aiming to refine your strategy, our club offers a welcoming environment for all.

Our History

Founded in 2024, the Rototuna Chess Club started as a small group of friends who shared a passion for chess. Over the years, we have grown into a vibrant community with members ranging from young students to experienced players. We take pride in our friendly and supportive atmosphere where members can enjoy the game and improve their skills.

Our Activities

We host a variety of activities throughout the year, including:

  • Weekly club meetings
  • Monthly tournaments
  • Workshops and training sessions
  • Community outreach programs
